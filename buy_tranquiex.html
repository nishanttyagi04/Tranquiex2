Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Tranquiex without the wristband?

While the core software works without the wristband, you'll miss out on the vibration alerts and offline protection features that make Tranquiex uniquely effective.

How does Tranquiex know I'm not in front of the screen?

Tranquiex uses advanced computer vision through your device's camera to detect presence and attention levels, ensuring you stay focused during study sessions.

Is it compatible with Mac and Windows?

Yes, Tranquiex is fully compatible with both Mac and Windows systems. The wristband connects seamlessly via Bluetooth on both platforms.

Can my parent disable a session remotely?

Yes, parents can use their Authenticator Code to remotely manage sessions, including pausing or ending them when needed.
